About Me

Hi, I'm Peter, a Construction Project Manager.

I graduated from BCIT Architectural and Building Engineering Technology in 2004 and have been working in the construction industry ever since. I have managed a variety of construction projects, including concrete high-rise, mid-rise, sports facility, mixed-use commercial/residential, airport facilities, office renovations, retail boutique, wood multi-family residential, industrial, commercial TI's (tenant improvements).

I have a passion for learning new skills and technologies, and that's why I'm currently learning coding and software development.

I want to leverage my knowledge of construction to build software solutions that can improve project management, communication, and collaboration in the industry. I'm always looking for a new challenge and opportunity to grow.
